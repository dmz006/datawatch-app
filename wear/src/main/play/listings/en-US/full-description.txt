datawatch Wear OS is the official smartwatch companion to the datawatch server — the open-source daemon that bridges
  AI coding sessions across machines.

  Monitor from your wrist:

  • See every active session across every datawatch server you run.
  • Reply to prompts by voice directly from your watch.
  • Stream live status, logs, and alerts to your Wear OS device.
  • Voice commands to trigger session actions without touching your phone.

  Built for developers on the move:

  • Wear OS tiles show active session count at a glance.
  • Complications for CPU, memory, and session counters on your watchface.
  • Voice notifications wake you when a session needs attention.
  • On-device transcription for hands-free replies.

  Secure and private:

  • No ads. No analytics. No tracking.
  • Bearer token secured in Android Keystore.
  • All push notifications encrypted and content-free.
  • Requires: an existing datawatch server you operate. Get datawatch at https://github.com/dmz006/datawatch
