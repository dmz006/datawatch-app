# ADR-0028 — Wear OS MVP Surfaces

## Status
Accepted (superseded in part by ADR-0042)

## Context
The Wear OS scope needed to be bounded to achievable MVP surfaces before Sprint 1 began.

## Decision
MVP Wear surfaces: ongoing notification (w1), watchface complication (w3), rich Wear app with dictation reply (w4). Tile (w2) deferred.

## Consequences
The Wear Tile (w2) was subsequently promoted to the v0.10.0 scope by ADR-0042, landing in Sprint 4.
